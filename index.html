I'm a Roboticist

I Develop Intelligent Aerial Robots that Help People

Hi, I'm Eugenio Cuniato, a Ph.D. student in the Autonomous Systems Lab at ETH Zürich. My research focuses on the development of high-performance aerial robots for safe physical interaction.

I am particularly interested in how we can use A.I. to fully develop the potential of these systems.

My Projects

  • Nowhere to hide anymore

    Learning to Open Doors with an Aerial Manipulator

    We use reinforcement learning to teach a drone to open doors. The drone learns to open doors in simulation and then applies what it learned to the real world.

    Eugenio Cuniato, Ismail Geles, Weixuan Zhang, Olov Andersson, Marco Tognon, Roland Siegwart

  • Safer aerial robots

    Power-based Safety Layer for Aerial Vehicles in Physical Interaction using Lyapunov Exponents

    We implement a safety layer inside our control architecture that guarantees the safety of the aerial robot during interaction with the environment. We won the Best Paper Award for this one!

    E. Cuniato, N. R. J. Lawrance, M. Tognon and R. Siegwart
